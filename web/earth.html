DAC|PlanetaryFormats(Γ):2019

Earth surface is defined here as a 510 065 605 km² plane with aspect ratio of 1:√2. This is the same ratio that is used in the ISO 216 paper size standard (the series "A", "B" and "C").

510 065 605 km² can be represented as a 18 991 × 26 858 km rectangle. Turned into millimeters and rounded to the nearest integrer, it define the first format named Γ0 after the first character of Gaïa in greek (Γαία).

This format has a width of 18 991 455 186 mm and a height of 26 857 973 494 mm.

This first approximation with a millimeter precision result in an error of a missing 1923.153920 m² surface.

This catalog presents on each page the folded format of the previous page format until we reach the first format that can be filled in a A4 which is the number 53 (a Γ0 folded in half by its larger side 53 times). The Γ53 is printed here at its real scale, the size of this catalog.
